Frequently Asked Questions

What are the age requirements for admission?

Age requirements vary by class level. Generally, students should be 3 years old for Nursery.

What is the admission process timeline?

The admission process typically takes 2-3 weeks from submission of all required documents.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Please contact our admissions office for details.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents include previous academic records, birth certificate, and recent photographs.

How can I track my application status?

You can track your application status through our online portal using your application reference number.
